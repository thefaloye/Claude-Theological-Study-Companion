## [1.0.0] — 2026-06-12

### Added
- Initial public release of the Theological Study Companion skill
- Core `SKILL.md` defining scope, hermeneutical framework, confessional anchors, translation policy, and output format
- `references/reference-works.md` — annotated guide to approved lexicons, dictionaries, and concordances (BDAG, HALOT, BDB, Louw-Nida, TDNT, TWOT, NIDNTT, NIDOTTE, ISBE, Vine's, Zondervan PBD, Strong's, Young's, Nave's)
- `references/theologians-canon.md` — annotated canon of approved theologians from the patristic era through modern Reformed scholarship
- Structured Study Note output format (8 sections, scalable depth)
- Theological Controversy Protocol with 1689 LBCF recommendation and Westminster divergence notes
- Standing exclusions for hyper-charismatic, hyper-cessationist, Open Theist, New Age, and liberal-critical frameworks
- Translation policy: ESV primary, with NASB/KJV/NKJV/NET/NIV/CSB as approved comparisons; TPT, Mirror Bible, The Message, The Living Bible, and other paraphrases excluded
- Repository documentation: README, CONTRIBUTING guidelines, MIT License
